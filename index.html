About

Our coffee shop is a full-service coffee shop and cafe. We offer quality coffee and espresso drinks as well as bakery items. Our baristas use coffee beans roasted right here in the great state of Michigan. We're also proud to support small farms by sourcing all of our ingredients locally. We are the perfect shop for you whether you're looking to stop by for a quick grab or want a great place to have a meet up with friends & family. We'll see you soon!
